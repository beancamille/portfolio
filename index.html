Research Interests

From June 2018 to May 2020, I carried out laboratory research as a part of my undergraduate education. My research was concentrated in experimental condensed matter physics, which is a field that focuses on the properties of matter as condensed materials (as opposed to individual particles). Most of the research that I completed was related to the fabrication and analysis of thin film materials.

Computer Science Experience

In my undergraduate studies, I completed courses in systems development, computer hardware, and computational science. All of my most significant coding projects were written as final projects for computer science coursework, and I have primarily coded in Python.
